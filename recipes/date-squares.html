Date Squares

(Image taken from The Salt and Sweet Kitchen)

Description

These date squares are perfect for an on-the-go snack or a quick, delicious breakfast.

They are bound together by a thick homemade date paste, chopped nuts, and chunks of coconut biscuits, topped with desiccated coconut.

These no-bake bars are perfect for kids and adults!

Ingredients

  • 125g butter
  • 1/8 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg, whisked
  • 250g dates,pitted
  • 1 packet (250g) coconut sables, crushed into small pieces
  • coconut, as desired
  • favourite nuts,optional

Steps

  1. Place the butter in a pot on the stove
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Stir the sugar into the melted butter
  4. Add the vanilla essence, cinnamon and dates. Stir constantly
  5. Heat the mixture until the dates are soft and gooey
  6. Remove from heat and add the crushed coconut sables and egg
  7. Add the extra coconut and nuts (if using)
  8. Press mixture into a lined baking tray
  9. Sprinkle extra coconut on top, if desired
  10. Refrigerate for 20 minutes
  11. Cut into squares
  12. Enjoy!
