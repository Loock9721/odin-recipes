Easy Mexican Mince Nachos

(Image taken from With a Blast)

Description

A flavourful dish containing mince, tomatoes and pinto beans (or black beans).

Other seasonings include cumin, garlic, coriander powder and coriander leaf (cilantro)>.

A great dish to share with friends!

This dish is not spicy, so everybody can enjoy it! That being said, spiceness can be adjusted according to taste.

This is a very easy dish to prepare!

Ingredients

Mince

(Tip! It is best to let the mince mixture stand for 24 hours)
  • 400g mince (I used pork mince)
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 50g tomato purée
  • 1 cube chicken/beef stock, with 250ml water
  • 1 can of pinto beans/black beans
  • 3 tsp paprika
  • 2 Tbsp garlic, minced
  • 3 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 4 Tbsp cumin
  • 3 Tbsp coriander leaf (cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

Guacamole

  • 1 large, ripe avocado (or 2 small ones)
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 6 drops Tabasco sauce
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • coriander leaf, as desired
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

Other

  • 1 packet nacho chips or cheese Doritos
  • 1 small tub of plain yoghurt or sour cream
  • Cheese, grated (as desired)

Steps

Mince

  1. Add the mince, purée, stock into a pot
  2. Brown the mince
  3. Add all the seasoning
  4. Cook the mixture for about 10 minutes
  5. Add the canned tomatoes and beans
  6. Stir well
  7. Simmer for 40 minutes, stirring every few minutes
  8. Turn off the heat
  9. Let stand for 24 hours, if possible

Guacamole

  1. Mash the avocado
  2. Add all the other ingredients
  3. Mix well

Assembly

  • Place nacho chips on a plate
  • Put mince on top of nachos
  • Melt cheese over mince and nachos
  • Spoon guacamole and yoghurt/sour cream onto each serving as desired
  • Enjoy!
